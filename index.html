About Unbound

Unbound by Himanshi is a soulful social initiative born from a quiet yet powerful realization ~ that in an age of growing awareness, education, and evolution, we are no longer meant to remain tethered to outdated patterns of living. It is a gentle invitation to release what no longer serves you ~ the unhealthy habits, the silent expectations, and the unseen pressures that ask you to fit into a life already defined.

At its heart, Unbound is a return to self. To be unbound is to set yourself free from limiting patterns and inherited conditioning, and to step into a life shaped by your own conscious choices. It is about living fully in the present, embracing your inner voice, and honoring the truth that we are independent, aware beings meant to experience life in our own unique way.

Unbound believes that true freedom is not loud or forceful, but deeply personal ~ it begins the moment you choose yourself.
A Message from the Founder
“Unbound was created from a deep need to bring light back into the way we live ~ a life where we are courageous enough to choose what we truly love, rather than quietly following definitions set by others.

It is about returning to what feels real ~ living with love, with values that genuinely resonate, with kindness, and with a sense of joy that is not conditioned by approval.

Unbound brings attention to the personal and professional spaces that often limit us ~ the patterns that cage us mentally and emotionally ~ and opens up a path toward living with greater awareness, choice, and truth.”
~ Himanshi Shrimali
